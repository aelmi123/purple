Purple-backed Thornbill

The purple-backed thornbill (Ramphomicron microrhynchum) is a species of hummingbird. It is found in Bolivia, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, and Venezuela

Population size: unknown Population trend: Decreasing Extent of occurrence (breeding/resident): 2,750,000 km2 LC on the extinction scale - least concern
